Flow chart to find a number is a prime or composite
1.Start
2.Accept a positive integer (number greater than 2)
3.Initialize counter with a value 2
4. Divide the number with the counter 
5.If the remainder is zero,display that "n"  is composite and stop the algoritim
6.else, increment the counter and repeat from step 4 untill the counter value is less than "n"
7.after completion of all the steps display that the number "n" is prime.
